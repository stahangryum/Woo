Useful Functions

reshape

Tip: R의 dim함수와 유사하다. 
A = np.array([11,22,33,44,55,66])
A

array([11, 22, 33, 44, 55, 66])
A.reshape(2,3)

array([[11, 22, 33],
       [44, 55, 66]])
A

array([11, 22, 33, 44, 55, 66])
A = A.reshape(2,3)
A

array([[11, 22, 33],
       [44, 55, 66]])

note :reshape with -1

A = np.arange(24)
A
